WHO WE ARE

With a variety of goods and services to fulfil the stringent and diverse needs of its customers, OGOT OVERSEAS TRADING is the industry leader in the oilfield and structural steel sectors. Carbon steel pipe Flanges, CS B.W Fittings, Forged Fittings, Stud Bolts & Gaskets, Galvanized pipe and Fittings, Steel plates, high grade plates, and marine plates, steel sections, valves, and SS goods are among the company's product offerings. Through its broad range of goods & services and with the assistance of a team of knowledgeable and committed specialists, OGOT OVERSEAS TRADING satisfies the high standards of its customers in the oil & gas sector, the petroleum chemical industry, and the construction sector. The company supplies a variety of items adapted for specific requirements, sourced from manufacturers all over the world, and continuous innovation is the company's slogan. Today, OGOT is a well-known stockist of oilfield and structural steel products in the area. By upholding the highest standards of quality and a dedication to excellence, the company continues to solidify its reputation in the oil field and structural steel industries
