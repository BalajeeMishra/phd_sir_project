Our Services

Oil & Gas Division.

Within this section, there are two primary product categories. Drilling package Supply: In this section, we keep a large inventory of and provide items for the oil and gas exploration and production industry, including manifolds, and OCTG, drill pipes and wellheads etc. Piping Package Supply: In this section we significantly stock and supply pipes, fittings flanges and accessories, both in carbon and stainless-steel material. We offer piping packages to industries including petrochemical, desalination, power, and refineries etc.

Agency Representation Division

Through our regional offices, we represent foreign producers, suppliers, and service providers in this division. OGOT was established in an effort to address the needs of the utilities, oil and gas, and petrochemical industries. Our service is interwoven with expertise, quality, and teamwork, which helps clients in the oil & gas and utilities sectors function at their best. We have concentrated on utilizing these resources to provide solutions that enhance customer performance since the beginning. Our skill and capability, supported by a vast inventory of manufacturers who have been approved by all of the main oil and gas companies have earned us a solid reputation as a fast-track supplier. Additionally, we provide complete piping work packages for the construction of chemical, power generation, desalination, marine, and offshore oil and gas, petrochemical, cryogenic, and construction facilities.

MEP Division

This branch meets the steel and piping needs of the district cooling, firefighting, and MEP (mechanical, electrical, and plumbing) industries. We keep a large supply of ERW Pipes, Galvanized Pipes, Grooved Pipes, Grooved Fittings, Valves, etc. from this Division in stock and supply.
